Donation Process

For making donation, you will have to click on the 'Donate Now' button given on home page.

Follow the steps given below to make donation successfully.

  1. When you click on the button, a payment box pops up. Fill amount that you want to donate and click on next.
  2. Fill the required fields with correct information and then click on 'Proceed to Pay'.
  3. Click the preferable payment type and pay click on pay button given below.
  4. Once you pay the amount, a message will will appear on your screen confirming if the payment was successful or not.
  5. You will also receive receipt of the transaction on your email id.
